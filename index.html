Financial Formulas

Return on Equity (ROE)

$$ ROE = \frac{Net\ Income}{Shareholders'\ Equity} $$

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

$$ CAGR = \left(\frac{V_f}{V_i}\right)^{\tfrac{1}{n}} - 1 $$

Give quick intuition: ROE measures efficiency; CAGR smooths multi-period growth.
